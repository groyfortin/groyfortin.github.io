Research

I am interested in geometric spectral theory. More precisely, I study growth and nodal sets of
eigenfunctions of elliptic operators on Riemannian manifolds.

Publications

    Ann. Sci. Math. Quebec 36 (2012), 169-178. arXiv:1007.4771.
